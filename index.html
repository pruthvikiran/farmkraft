Welcome

Who We are

We “Swastik Farm Krafts” are a Partnership based company, Incorporated in the year 1971 at Bengaluru, Karnataka,engaged as the manufacturer and wholesaler of Grass Cutting Machine, Grass Cutter, Single Blade Hedge Trimmer, Earth Auger and many more. The complete assortment is manufactured utilizing qualitative raw material and higher techniques, which have made them best in terms of consistency and effectiveness.

Our Mission

Provide agricultural machinery, implements and irrigation equipment that combines quality performance with value pricing thereby increasing quality and productivity in the agricultural sector of the country.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the first choice in marketing the highest quality agricultural equipment and providing superior product support, High-tech solutions for farmers feeding the world.

Our Values

We are a customer-oriented company and believe in developing strong, positive and longlasting relationships with all our customers,We are dedicated towards providing goods and services of excellent quality with a competitive prices to our entire client base.

Our Infrastructure

Since the commencement of our firm, we have been capable of providing the need of our customers. Our processing unit functions with the massive support of modern machines and equipment giving great thrust to all business activities.
